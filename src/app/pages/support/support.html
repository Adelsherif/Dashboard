Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

Go to the “Products” tab and click on the “Add Product” button. Fill in all required fields and click save.

Can I edit or delete an existing order?

Yes, navigate to the “Orders” section. You can edit details or delete orders depending on user permissions.

How do I reset my password?

Click on your profile picture in the top right corner, then select “Account Settings” → “Change Password”.
